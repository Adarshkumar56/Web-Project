Introduction

Welcome to Dps Global

Dps Global has been established with the aim of nurturing young minds by offering them an environment full of positivity and happiness. We describe ourselves as place , where social, emotional, intellectual and physical development takes place in a stress free and happy environment.

Our innovative teaching aid, helps children fall in love with the learning process and develop a positive outlook in life… which is important for the leaders of tomorrow.

The highly committed team at Dps Global firmly believe that pre schooling is not just a business but an investment towards the future of our nation.

We are committed towards hand holding our partners in establishing an easy to manage set up, which can be started from home involving limited resources and easy returns.

Our well networked business model coupled with goodwill has helped us grow our footprints rapidly across the country.

We are looking for partners who are willing to take our mission forward, bring about a positive change in our society and are willing to invest their time and energy in maintaining the standards of Dps Global. We believe in maintaining the highest standards and expect the same from our partners.

We appreciate your keen interest in our offerings and request you to attend a meeting at our corporate or branch office.

Eligibility for Master Franchisen

Taking the responsibility of managing Dps Global branches is a lifetime

opportunity. We are looking for the following qualities in our partners:-

a) Passion towards the education industry with a role that has had an impact in the society.
b) Willingness to create a world of difference for oneself and for the society as a whole.
c) Having a total turnover of Rs 1 crore.
d) Having an office in an urban set up at prime location in any major city/state capital in India.
e) Having a minimum office space of 1000 sq feet with minimum 8 to 10 seated conference room.
f) The company or individual should not have any criminal cases against itself/ himself or any of his employees.
